# Video Directory

This folder should contain your sound system demonstration video.

## Required Video

- **Filename**: `sound-system-demo.mp4`
- **Format**: MP4 (recommended)
- **Recommended specs**:
  - Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
  - Duration: 1-3 minutes
  - File size: Under 50MB for web performance

## How to Add Your Video

1. Record a video showing your Noshahi Sound System in action at an event
2. Show the equipment setup, sound quality, and professional service
3. Save/export the video as `sound-system-demo.mp4`
4. Place the file in this `videos` folder
5. The video will automatically appear on the Showcase page

## Temporary Placeholder

Until you add your video, the showcase page will display a poster image with a play button overlay. Click the play button to test the video controls.

## Tips for a Great Video

- Show the system at a real event
- Include satisfied clients or guests
- Demonstrate sound quality and clarity
- Highlight professional setup and service
- Keep it engaging and under 3 minutes
